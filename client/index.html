Welcome to Trails.lol

Nick name must be 15 alphanumeric characters or less.

Instructions:

  1. Arrow keys to turn.
  2. Space bar to teleport.
  3. Other trails kill you.
  4. Avoid your own trails.
  5. Become larger with more points, but also slower.

Power ups:

  1. Green: lose less points on your trail.
  2. Red: gain and lose points faster.
  3. Orange: longer teleport range
  4. Yellow: shorter teleport cooldown.
  5. Blue: larger teleport clearing effect.
  6. Purple: Move faster.

Some combinations will modify your space bar ability.
